How a deposit moves

Three steps, one transaction to start.

STEP / DEPOSIT

You deposit once

Send any supported asset to a vault. You receive a receipt token (ovETH, ovUSDC) representing your share — it grows in value as yield accrues, instead of issuing separate reward tokens to claim.

STEP / ROUTE

Strategy routes it

The vault's strategy contract puts capital to work — staking, lending, or providing liquidity, depending on the vault. Routing logic and risk parameters are fixed at deployment and published on-chain.

STEP / COMPOUND

Yield reinvests itself

Earned yield is harvested and swapped back into principal automatically on a schedule, not manually. Your share token's exchange rate ticks up — withdraw any time, no lockups, no claiming gas.

Audited, not just announced

Every strategy contract is reviewed before deposits open, and again after any parameter change. Reports are published in full, not summarized.
